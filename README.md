## ğŸ¯ Game Features

### Core Gameplay
- **Turn-based baseball**: Simple but engaging batting and pitching mechanics
- **Team management**: Manage 15 players with different skills and positions
- **Budget system**: Earn money from wins to improve your team

- **Procedural generation**: Each season features randomized stadiums and events

- **Fenway Park**: Short left field wall boosts home runs
- **Coors Field**: Thin air increases ball carry
- **Minute Maid Park**: Tall wall in left center
- **Yankee Stadium**: Short right field porch
- **Petco Park**: Pitcher-friendly dimensions

### Weather Effects
- **Clear**: Normal gameplay
- **Rainy**: Reduced hitting performance
- **Cold**: Slightly reduced performance
- **Foggy**: Reduced visibility affects gameplay
- **Windy**: Increased ball carry
